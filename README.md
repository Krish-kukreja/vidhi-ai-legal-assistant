# VIDHI - Voice-Integrated Defense for Holistic Inclusion

**AI-Powered Legal Assistant for Indian Citizens & Enterprise Professionals**

VIDHI is a comprehensive legal tech platform designed to bridge the justice gap in India. It empowers ordinary citizens with accessible legal guidance while equipping professional lawyers with powerful AI-driven contract redlining and case matter management tools. Supported in 22+ Indian languages with full voice capabilities, VIDHI makes the law accessible to everyone.

## 🚀 Key Capabilities

### 🏛️ For Citizens
- **22+ Indian Languages** - Interact natively in Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, and more.
- **Voice-First Design** - Speak your questions and receive audio responses in your local dialect.
- **Emergency Legal SOS** - Immediate, actionable rights information (D.K. Basu guidelines) for urgent situations like police detentions.
- **Government Scheme & Subsidy Finder** - Discover and navigate complex government schemes seamlessly.

### 💼 For Legal Professionals (Enterprise)
- **AI Contract Redlining** - Upload complex contracts and let our AWS Bedrock models extract high, medium, and low severity risks directly aligned with your firm's custom playbook.
- **Matter Workspaces** - Securely segment chat history, documents, and research into isolated client case folders.
- **Document Education System** - Interactive teaching UI for breaking down heavy legal documents clause-by-clause.
- **Live Case Law Research** - Instant, hallucination-free synthesis of the latest appellate rulings and acts.

## 🌐 Supported Languages (Voice & Text)

- **Premium Neural Voice (Text-to-Speech)**: Hindi (hi-IN), Bengali (bn-IN), English (en-IN), Tamil (ta-IN), Telugu (te-IN), Malayalam (ml-IN), Kannada (kn-IN).
- **Speech Recognition (Audio-to-Text)**: Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, English.
- **Regional Dialects**: Bhojpuri, Maithili, Awadhi.

## 🎯 Our Mission

Built specifically for the intricacies of the Indian Legal System, VIDHI's mission is to democratize legal aid. By leveraging cloud-native Large Language Models and culturally localized voice interfaces, we ensure that specialized legal knowledge—from constitutional rights to complex corporate contracts—is never out of reach.
